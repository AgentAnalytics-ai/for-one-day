For One Day · Internal whiteboard · June 2026

Kitchen wall — every page

One household daily plan at home. Each surface has a phase: what ships now, what it must feel like (2027 psychology), and what is explicitly out. No scrolling on the wall. No empty cards. No “Nothing scheduled.”

North star: Kitchen tablet = glance in 3 seconds, tap to act. Phone = save photos & depth in More. Calendar = read from Google; meals & lists = edit here.

Calendar truth (avoid confusion)

This week does not create Google events. Grant & Sara add future events in Google Calendar (or phone calendar). For One Day merges and shows them. Meals and lists are edited in-app. Say this once in This week subtitle — not on Today.

Alignment check

Question we asked

Can AI read the link and write a better recipe?

Aligned answer: AI already writes tonight’s playbook from name + notes. Link stays a bookmark. “Better” = right for your home tonight, not beating the blogger. URL read is optional tier 3 later — not the foundation.

Question we asked

Save Pinterest without copy-paste every time?

Aligned answer: Yes — 6E saved chips. Save once, pick many. Not the same as Pinterest living inside our app (that’s out of scope).
