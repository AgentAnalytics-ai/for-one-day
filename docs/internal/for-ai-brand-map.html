For One Day · Internal

Invisible help

The best AI in daily life doesn’t announce itself. Users should feel guided, not assisted by AI. One brand: For One Day. No forced sub-names like ForDinner or ForPlanning.

Rule: Verbs on the UI. Intelligence in the backend. If Sara wouldn’t say it at the stove, it doesn’t ship.
